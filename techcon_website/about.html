Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small meetup for local developers in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for showcasing technological innovation and connecting thought leaders from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, empower collaboration, and accelerate the future of technology. We bring together developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, and tech enthusiasts to learn, share, and create solutions that drive progress.
